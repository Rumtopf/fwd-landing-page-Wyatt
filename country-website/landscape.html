Landscaping

landscaping on an acre of land which was mostly a hill was a consistent challenge against nature. large trees provided a lot of shady area's. so gardnening had to happen around where the sun would peak through The home itself was built on black granite rock that was blasted and duumped all over the yard, parts of which you will see in the gallery pictures.

The Coy Pond!

the pond, nestled between the house and garage, was built into the old driveway with pickaxe, shovel wheelbarrow amd a just a little bit of hardwork. the proccess took a few months but as you can see what we got out of it was well worth it! Photoraphy courtesy of Wyatt Spratt

The Rockery!

the rockery or rockeries we had were an upper rockery and a lower rockery. I moved most of those rocks by hand my self. Black granite is a really dense rock and is quite heavy Some of the bigger rocks that I moved around the property were around 300-450lbs yes these were moved by hand. Only moving them via wheelbarrow when they neeed to be moved across the yard. Photoraphy courtesy of Wyatt Spratt
